Simple Local Storage

1. Write something

Press Save to save your text to local storage

2. Refresh page (cmd+R on mac)

You'll see your text disappear. (It's still in the local storage though.

3. Press Get Text

This grabs it from the local storage and puts it back again.

4. Clear

This clears local storage.

You can store 1 save at a time. Pressing save multiple times overwrites.
